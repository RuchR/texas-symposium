The steering committee invites paper submissions for the 2017 conference, to be held on the beautiful campus of Baylor University in Waco, Texas. Papers will be archived and published in the conference proceedings and at IEEE Xplore. Paper submissions in all areas of wireless and microwave circuits and systems are welcome. Topics of interest include, but are not limited to, the following:

  • • RF and microwave circuits, devices, modeling, testing, and measurement
  • • Radar and sensing
  • • Antennas, propagation, and electromagnetics
  • • Microwave applied metrology
  • • Wireless power transfer
  • • Electromagnetic materials and modeling
  • • Wireless or optical communication
  • • Wireless technologies for biomedical applications
  • • Internet of Things
  • • Emerging wireless technologies
